Drink Visualizer

The Drink Visualizer is an intuitive way to show drinks with their core ingredients. By interpreting the information sent by the coffeetrainerAPI, the visualizer can generate a picture that shows exactly what percentage of the drink is sugary syrup and what part is coffee. This will help both baristas and customers get a better understanding of what is going to cups.

When making these drinks, don't forget to add the love and care by hand from the heart!

POS Simulator

Using the app, baristas can effectively: learn how to take actual customer orders, become familiar with the layout of the POS system, and become more comfortable asking questions to customers so they can make the moment right with every order. This project takes in many parameters to wholly simulate on-the-job scenarios, so partners feel comfortable before their first day on the floor.

Roasts

The roasts section is a collection of all the Starbucks™ core and seasonal roasts. Complete with notes, flavors, and growing regions, the roasts page has all the informational building blocks to start your journey as a coffee master or to "bring your best" to your next coffee tasting.
